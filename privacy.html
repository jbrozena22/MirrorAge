Summary: MemoryAge is designed with privacy as a priority. Your data never leaves your device. We do not use cloud servers, analytics, or third-party tracking.

1. Overview

MemoryAge ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our iOS application handles your data. Our business model is simple: we provide a utility tool for parents, not a data collection service.

2. Data Collection & Storage

We believe that your family's data belongs to you. As such, all data is stored locally on your device.

Information we process locally:

  • Children's Names & Birth Dates: You enter this information manually to allow the app to calculate age comparisons. This data is stored in your device's local storage (UserDefaults) and is never transmitted to us.
  • Photo & Video Metadata: The app scans the creation dates of your photos and videos to find matches. This processing happens entirely on your device's processor.

3. Permissions

To function, MemoryAge requires specific permissions on your iOS device:

  • Photo Library Access: We request access to your photo library to find videos of your older child that match the age of your younger child.
    Note: While the app requests Read/Write access (to potentially allow saving future creations), we primarily use Read access to analyze timestamps. We do not upload your photos or videos to any server.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your iOS Settings app.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services that collect data.

  • No Analytics: We do not track how you use the app.
  • No Advertising: We do not display ads or share data with ad networks.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not have a backend server. Your data stays on your phone.
