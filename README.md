# MirrorAge

**MirrorAge** is a way to compare your child's growth and milestones across different ages, helping you preserve and reflect on precious memories as your little one grows.

## About

MirrorAge allows you to compare photos and memories of your child at different ages side by side. Simply select photos from different time periods to see how your child has grown and changed over time. It's a simple, beautiful way to capture and celebrate the journey of childhood.

## Features

- **Side-by-Side Comparison**: Easily compare photos of your child at different ages
- **Memory Organization**: Organize photos by date and age to track milestones
- **Simple & Intuitive**: Clean interface designed to focus on what matters - your memories
- **Privacy First**: Your photos and data are stored locally on your device

## Getting Started

1. Download MirrorAge from the App Store
2. Start adding photos of your child
3. Compare photos side by side to see how they've grown
