MirrorAge Support

MirrorAge is a way to compare your child's growth and milestones across different ages, helping you preserve and reflect on precious memories as your little one grows.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use MirrorAge?

MirrorAge allows you to compare photos and memories of your child at different ages side by side. Simply select photos from different time periods to see how your child has grown and changed over time.

How do I add photos?

To add photos, tap the add button in the app and select images from your photo library. You can organize photos by date and age to easily compare different periods.

Can I edit or delete photos?

Yes, you can edit or delete any photos you've added. Simply select the photo and use the edit or delete options available.

Is my data secure?

Your photos and data are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit your personal information. Be sure to back up your device regularly to keep your memories safe.

What devices are supported?

MirrorAge is designed for iOS devices. Please ensure you're running a compatible version of iOS for the best experience.

The app is not working correctly. What should I do?

If you're experiencing issues:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of MirrorAge installed
  • Restart the app by closing it completely and reopening it
  • Restart your device
  • Check that you have sufficient storage space available
  • If the problem persists, please contact support with details about the issue
